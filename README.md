## Overview

The **Autonomous Factory** workshop brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders to explore how agentic AI systems are reshaping modern manufacturing — from intelligent design and planning to fully automated production workflows.

We invite submissions on topics including but not limited to:

- **Agentic AI Systems** &mdash; LLM-based agents for autonomous decision-making in industrial settings
- **Intelligent Design & Planning** &mdash; AI-driven product design, process planning, and optimization
- **Workflow Automation** &mdash; End-to-end automation of manufacturing pipelines using foundation models
- **Digital Twins & Simulation** &mdash; AI-powered virtual replicas for predictive manufacturing
- **Quality Control & Inspection** &mdash; Vision and language models for automated quality assurance
- **Human-AI Collaboration** &mdash; Augmenting human expertise with agentic AI on the factory floor
